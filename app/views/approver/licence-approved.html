If changes are made

The probation practitioner can make changes to reporting instructions through the Create and vary a licence service. These changes do not need to be reapproved by the prison, but a case administrator may need to reprint the licence.

To change the curfew address, curfew hours or first night curfew hours the probation practitioner should email createandvaryalicence@digital.justice.gov.uk.
