Help with this licence condition

If curfews are longer than 12 hours
You must use a bespoke licence condition approved by PPCS if:
  • the total length of someone's curfews is more than 12 hours
  • they need more than 3 curfews
If you are also adding reporting instructions
Add an hour to the total length of someone's curfews each time you ask them to report to a police station or approved premises in an additional licence condition.

If this total is more than 12 hours, you must use a bespoke condition approved by PPCS.

For example, if someone had an 11-hour curfew and had to report to a police station twice a day, the total length of their curfews would be 13 hours. This would need PPCS approval.
