MVP2

We will work with our partner prison to select approximately 10 cases coming up for release to test our MVP2 flow.

MVP2 case criteria
  • Prisoner has already been approved for HDC, therefore a HDCAD already exists
  • It's well in advance of their CRD
  • Can have standard, additional or bespoke licence conditions
  • 1 release address that can be CAS or residential - but address must be known
  • 1 curfew per day
  • Can be standard curfew hours or different (COM will enter these)
  • Standard 1st night curfew of 3pm-7am
