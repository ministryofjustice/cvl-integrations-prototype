MVP2

We will work with our partner prison to select approximately 10 cases coming up for release to test our MVP2 flow.

MVP2 case criteria
  • Prisoner has already been approved for HDC, therefore a HDCAD already exists
  • It's well in advance of their CRD
  • Can have standard, additional or bespoke licence conditions
  • 1 release address that can be CAS or residential - but address must be known
  • 1 curfew per day
  • Can be standard curfew hours or different (COM will enter these)
  • Standard 1st night curfew of 3pm-7am

Prototype for testing w/c 19 February 2025

Journey 1 - Guy Richards

Scenario: Create licence with standard curfew hours and the 3 licence conditions. Then edit that same licence to change curfew hours to 7pm-6am, and resubmit to the prison.

Start

Journey 2 - Diego Rodriguez

Scenario: Vary the person's licence to change curfew hours so they're shorter Monday-Friday, and submit this to the PDU Head. Also have many conditions listed. Also fast forward to post-approval setting licence to active and printing the new licence.

Start

Journey 3 - Montgomery Talbot

Scenario: Vary the person's licence to change their address. Address is suitable, offline process completed, but they still need to submit this to the PDU Head. Also fast forward to post-approval setting licence to active and printing the new licence.
